How to Delete Your AIKA Account

Steps to Delete Your Account

  1. Login to your AIKA account using your credentials.
  2. Navigate to your profile settings by clicking on your profile icon.
  3. Within the profile settings, locate the 'Delete Account' option. Click on it to initiate the account deletion process.

By following these simple steps, you can securely delete your AIKA account.
